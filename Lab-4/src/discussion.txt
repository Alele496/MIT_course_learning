4. In your design of the clear method in mkMyCFFifo, how did you force the
    scheduling constraint {enq, deq} < clear?
    Answer: I used EHR port ordering: all state is stored in `Ehr#(2, ...)` registers. Normal operations (enq/deq) write to port 0, while `clear` writes to port 1. 
        Since port 1 has higher priority than port 0 in the EHR conflict matrix, clear always overrides concurrent enq/deq operations, enforcing the required scheduling constraint.
